By-Products

Recommended Business Structure

Limited Liability Company (LLC)

Optimal business structure for a pineapple company in Kenya

Advantages:
  • Separate legal entity providing personal asset protection
  • Ability to attract investors through share issuance
  • Enhanced credibility with banks and partners
  • Favorable tax planning opportunities
Considerations:
  • More paperwork and record-keeping
  • Higher setup and maintenance costs
  • Compliance with company regulations
  • Annual filing requirements

Why Invest in Golden Crown Pineapples?

  • Kenya's pineapple export market has grown by 299% over 5 years, with increasing demand in EU, Middle East, and regional markets.
  • Circular economy approach utilizing all parts of the pineapple, minimizing waste and maximizing revenue streams while supporting environmental sustainability.
  • Projected 25-30% profit margins by Year 5 with diversified revenue streams. Break-even expected by Year 3 with continuous growth potential.
  • Supporting local farmers through outgrower schemes, creating rural employment, and implementing sustainable agricultural practices that benefit the entire community.
